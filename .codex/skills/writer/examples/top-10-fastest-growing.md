### Methodology

Our growth score evaluates companies across four dimensions:

1. **Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)** - Absolute revenue scale
2. **Growth Rate** - Year-over-year revenue multiplier
3. **Funding Velocity** - Capital raised per month since founding
4. **Valuation Growth** - Percentage increase from first to latest funding round

We weighted these factors (40% ARR, 30% growth rate, 20% funding velocity, 10% valuation growth) to create a composite growth score. All data sourced from company disclosures, funding announcements, and market intelligence as of January 2026.

## Key Insights from the Data

### 1. Vertical Focus Beats Horizontal Breadth (For Now)

**Toma** (138x growth, automotive) and **Listen Labs** (15x growth, market research) prove that deep vertical expertise can outpace horizontal platforms in the short term. When you solve a specific problem deeply, adoption is faster and ROI is clearer.

### 2. Developer Tools Dominate Hypergrowth

6 of the top 10 companies build for developers:
- Cursor (code editor)
- Linear (engineering tools)
- Replit (coding platform)
- Anthropic (developer APIs)
- Perplexity (search/research)
- Vercel (deployment)

Developers adopt new tools faster than any other audience. They value quality, speed, and craft — and they're willing to pay premium prices.

### 3. Funding Velocity ≠ Growth Rate

**xAI** raises $1.24B/month but has the slowest revenue growth in the top 10. **Toma** raises almost nothing ($17M total) but grows 138x YoY.

Lesson: Capital enables scale, but product-market fit drives growth.
